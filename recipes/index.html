Apple Crisp

Apple crisp is a warm dessert made with sliced apples and a sweet, crumbly topping.

This classic treat combines the natural sweetness of fresh apples with a crunchy, golden crust made from oats, brown sugar, and butter. Perfect served warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream. Ideal for cozy nights and holiday gatherings!
