Come Unto Christ Bingo

How to play:

  1. Mingle and find others who match the description in a square.
  2. Once they find someone who fits, that person signs the square.
  3. Each person can only sign one square per card.
  4. First 5 persons to get 5 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) shouts “Come Unto Christ!” and wins.
