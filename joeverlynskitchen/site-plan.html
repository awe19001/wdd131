Joeverlyn Awe

WDD 131 . A1

Overview

Purpose

To share simple, delicious, and budget-friendly recipes with beginner cooks and students. The site will include easy instructions, helpful tips, and food photos to make cooking more enjoyable.

Audience

  • Beginner home cooks
  • Students living on their own
  • People looking for easy, quick meals
  • Anyone interested in learning the basics of cooking

    • Dynamic elements

  • JavaScript Module: Loads recipe data from recipes.mjs
  • DOM Interaction: Show/hide recipe details or tips when a button is clicked
  • Conditionals: Display a message if no recipe data is found
  • Arrays and Objects: Store and loop through recipes using forEach()
  • Event Listeners: Used for interactive recipe cards or tips
